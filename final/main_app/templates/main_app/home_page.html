What is NCA?

National Cyber Security Authority, or the Saudi National Cybersecurity Authority, is a government security entity in Saudi Arabia focusing primarily on the country's computer security. Created in 2017, it is directly linked to the office of the king.

Our Services

  • Audit Case Management: Manage and comply with multiple companies.
  • NCA Tree: Virtualizing and simplifying the audit process.
  • Recommendation: Recommended actions and tools for compliance.
